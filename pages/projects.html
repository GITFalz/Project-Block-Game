Project

This project began in early September of 2024, and I’ve been working on it whenever possible since then.

The goal is to develop a voxel game engine that empowers players to design their own “game” using a custom node-based system.

The engine will include built-in editors for creating blocks, trees, terrain, monsters, structures, and more. Players will have the freedom to craft expansive landscapes, from intricate, procedurally generated terrain to sprawling cities floating in the sky with ease. The vision is to enable players to build and share their unique worlds with friends, fostering creativity and collaboration.

With this type of world creation the player will be able to create their own unique world and still be able to explore it without being bored, and if he does get bored he can just download “WorldPacks” made by other people!
